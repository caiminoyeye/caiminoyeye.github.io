App Intra-city Book Borrowing:


Many people are interested in a lot of books,but they cannot afford them, so they are looking for ones who can exchange books. While some other people who own many books also search for books in different fields or books they don't own. Borrowing books in the same city,can help you identify like-minded friends and discuss your thoughts on these books with them. It also offers a good opportunity to tidy your collections at home and avoids wasting resources.
