Swan Posters:


If there is a black spot on a piece of white paper, people hate that the spot spoils the purity of the white paper. If there are two swans on the lake, one white and the other black, people feel they represent justice and evil, light and darkness. Because people are always longing for brightness, they like the beautiful, sparkling feathers of the white swan. In my mind, the black swan and the white swan are only different by a way of thinking. A white swan should endure slander and harm, and a black swan should be confident to open its wings and dance. No matter you are like the black swan or the white one, you change your mind, and your life will change.
